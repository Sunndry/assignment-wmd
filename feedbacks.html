Black Adam , June 2 By Wolfman

As a comic book lover, I appreciated this flick. I agree that we are in a superhero rut...but this film was both entertaining and faithful to the comics. Black Adam has always been a powerful (mainly villainous) force in the DC Universe, and this film depicted this very realistically, in my opinion. The Rock was a fantastic Black Adam! Hawkman, Dr. Fate, and the others were all fantastic! The villain could have been a little more terrifying (like Steppenwolf was in The Justice League), but overall I enjoyed the film and thought it was beautifully crafted and portrayed Black Adam in a terrific and enjoyable way. Overall, an enjoyable film!
